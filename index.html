About [XI] Xtraordinary

Welcome to [XI] Xtraordinary, one of the most competitive and dedicated teams on Nitro Type! We are a community of passionate typers who strive for excellence in every race.

Founded with a vision to create a team that embodies both skill and sportsmanship, [XI] Xtraordinary has grown into a powerhouse in the Nitro Type community. Our members range from casual racers to competitive champions, all united by our love for speed typing.

We believe in continuous improvement, teamwork, and maintaining a positive gaming environment. Whether you're a beginner looking to improve or an experienced typist seeking competition, [XI] Xtraordinary welcomes you to join our ranks!
